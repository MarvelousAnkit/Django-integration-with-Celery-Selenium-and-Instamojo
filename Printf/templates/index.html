Our Story

Printf was started by a group of B.Tech students from Amity University Patna who noticed the struggles of their peers when it came to printing out documents. They wanted to create an easier and more convenient way for students to get their printouts without the hassle of going out of their way.

With Printf, they are able to access a range of printers and have documents printed in just a few clicks with no need for physical contact. Printf is revolutionizing printing services, making it easy and hassle-free for students to get their documents printed quickly and efficiently..
